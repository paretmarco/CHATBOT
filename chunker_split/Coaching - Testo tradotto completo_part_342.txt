Method 2 – Self-magnetism This second method we are going to describe here is particularly suitable to the quest for a character transformation of our selves. It might however be also resorted to in order to accomplish physical results. Preparatory phase Decision and Consciousness of what one wants for himself Place yourselves in the present. You might possibly practice some exercise like the ones we have described in the first part of our treatment of the subject of becoming re-integrated with the now. As soon as we are going to be upon a firm point, we can then be able to create. Global declaration You must tell yourselves that the exercise is going to work. Such an asseveration is going to set the tone of your mind.
