Handling Identity Dreams 1. Acknowledge. Watch the mind acknowledge different stories and themes that arise. 2. Notice. Notice, for example, (a) rags to riches: seeing oneself arising from an impoverished position to be- come recognized and acknowledged (Rocky-type movies); (b) Cinderella complex: dreaming someone will materialize and take you "away from all this"; (c) seeing self as a football or rock star, or an influential person. Notice the story line; what is usually seen in these identity dreams is a one-dimensional replica of the story.
