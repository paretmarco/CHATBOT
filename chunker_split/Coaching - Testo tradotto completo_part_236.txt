Health and vigour are the indispensable prerequisite of every activity, be it intellectual or moral. It is only physical wellbeing which allows us to dispose of our time, as the latter is the unifying thread which life is made of. Health lies at the basis of energy, and this is, accordingly, the element we ought to focus upon. What we mean by state of health is a state which allows us to have ardent desires, that is, to direct ourselves, with covetous passion, towards that point where we become able to release our surplus energy.
