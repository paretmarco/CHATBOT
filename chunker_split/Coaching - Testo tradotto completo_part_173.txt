In addition to organizing our life so as to enable us to take a specific direction, practical ideation, which is the subject matter we are going to devote the present chapter to, appears after a while to have an impact on the unexpected as well. Even if the occurrence of such an impact is not an invariable phenomenon, we might nevertheless assert that the unexpected will at least be different from what it would have otherwise been had our actions not been premeditated. Life is but a single day The ultimate aim of our method is to teach the art of living in consonance with one’s natural talent at the loftiest and noblest level which can be achieved. An important rule, which complements the cardinal rule of Elimination, namely, «Do not do (say, think, feel) anything useless», is the cardinal rule of Organization: "The useful should be carried out (uttered, thought, felt) to the highest degree of perfection".
