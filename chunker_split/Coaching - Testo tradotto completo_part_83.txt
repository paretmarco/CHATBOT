Exercise 7 – Speaking When you speak, you should speak with the diaphragm rather than with the muscles of the throat. Never raise your voice, as that is after all the best way to dominate emotion and display self-control. Exercise 8 - Walking When you walk, you should make sure that you only feel the weight of your body on the feet, and not on the entire body. Exercise 9 – How to read and listen Whenever you are studying or listening to a speech or to some music, do not study or listen with your muscles, your spine, your shoulders or your face, but rather with the brain, which alone must stay alive. Put it in different words, you must relax the useless muscles. By so doing, you will realize that your attention, and your effectiveness, too, will be greater. You will in fact learn more, and strain yourselves less.
