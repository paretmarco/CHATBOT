The one who comes up with the word “to love” at the time of answering the question referred to in the description of the preceding exercise, might probably want to inject love into his work, and have a preference for activities which involve adding value to other people and enable a form of exchange, with the result that he will find his self-development in them. We accordingly enjoy a very extensive degree of freedom in defining these ideal professions and activities, always bearing in mind to somehow find an outlet of expression for the primal impulse which has set us onto our own specific directions. Life’s events and circumstances are opportunities which facilitate progression on the path, so long as the wayfarer’s attitude is the right one.
