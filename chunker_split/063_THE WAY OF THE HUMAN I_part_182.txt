GUILT A person once asked me, "How do you deal with guilt?" I said, "First you have to know what guilt is." In Gestalt therapy, for instance, guilt is resentment turned against yourself. So first you have to straighten out the energy. In other words, if I resent somebody or something, and I don't express that resentment, it's retroflected back' on me. That means that rather than expressing my resentment, I put it back on myself and what I get is guilt.
