We need the ability to question and be resilient. These are not just childhood traits, they are human traits, essential to fully functioning human beings. By consciously choosing to reform our childhood strategies for survival (the trances of the inner child), we can gain access to emotions which comprise our "delayed grief' and fully experience them. This allows us to integrate these crucial functions into our experiences, making us more fully present to live our own lives. You may find it tough going at times, and I urge you to hang in there. The mental stretch has been well worth the effort for me.
