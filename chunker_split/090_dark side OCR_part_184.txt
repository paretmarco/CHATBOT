Ask the inner child questions like, "What can you (the inner child) remember about X" (a particular situa- tion). For example, if you have an idea that your father drank, ask the inner child, "What can you (inner child) not remember about father's drinking?" or "What do you (the inner child) not know about your childhood?" Write down your answers and keep asking the question, until nothing pops-up. Examples for remembering the past: "What do you (the inner child) not know about X?"
