The child within a man was telling me he was afraid to make Appendix I . 159 changes in his life. The word change scared him. Change was fused with fear. I began talking about changing clothes to begin the process of creating new meanings for the word change. I talked about changing position when he made love with his wife so he could... feel more comfortable, "When you are making love, part of the time you are on your back and sometimes you are sitting up— but you can ...make those changes and ...feel more comfortable and find a more comfortable position for your legs or arms. Sometimes it can...be even more exciting to explore different positions to change into." I talked about making change in your pocket, chang- ing underwear, etc. The key word—change—needed to have many meanings. It is very important for all of us to notice how we limit ourselves by limiting the meanings of words.
