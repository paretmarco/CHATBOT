Our soul is in need of nourishment just as much as our body. A lovely sunset, the starred canopy of a sky, the contemplation of a beautiful building, all enrich whoever is capable of understanding such sceneries. Dreaming is by no means the same as wasting one’s time. Even the capability of having nice dreams is a wealth which cannot be acquired through money. If you were to study the mystery of the world inscribed in golden hieroglyphics on the vaults of the Empyrean, while feeling yourselves citizens of the Universe, you will experience things more than the person who only has the trivial ambition his tiny individual identity delineates. Do not raise the objection that you have too many matters to attend to for you to bother about casting a look at the sky or at the artistic world. Whoever only attends to his «routine» only lives in the middle of things: He is a poor fellow, even though possessed of very extensive material riches.
