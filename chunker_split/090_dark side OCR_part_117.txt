Step VI: Observe each voice, and notice that you, as the observer, exist with or without the voices. This is important to note, because often the only way a child can exist as a separate individual is to resist Mom or Dad. This rebel or outsider identity gets formed. This is difficult to give up because the person imagines that without the rebel or outsider, he would not exist, be annihilated, or disappear. Once the observer is established, letting go of old identities becomes easier. This is because there is a you beyond the rebel or outsider with whom you can identify.
