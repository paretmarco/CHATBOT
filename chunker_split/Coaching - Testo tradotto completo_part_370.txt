The mental attitude plays an essential role. You ought to feel that the universe is one, and it is accordingly as if, around your persons, an invisible enveloping agent were to deploy itself, an agent the main function of which consists in recording the targets of your volitional urges and harmonizing them with the natural elements so as to ensure their concrete actualizations. As previously emphasized, do not solely aim at achieving material objectives.
