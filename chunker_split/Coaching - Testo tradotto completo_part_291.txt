Many people are fastidiously precise only on condition of spending long periods of time on some work; instead, one must be able to quickly follow up an undertaking with some other one. Such ability is attained by premeditating actions and their sequence properly, as well by proceeding at a swift pace. We should in fact remember that every thought is action, it is movement, just as every movement is thought. It ensues from it that we need to act in conformity with a «vigil and rapid», vital rhythm, as opposed to one which is torpid and somnolent.
