Whoever, after all, desires to become an adept of the method laid out in this book will never run the risk of falling into an exclusive and noxious automatism. The method does indeed include one part which is daily consecrated to the gymnastics of the will, to exercising critical judgment. In actual fact, from it — once the signification of critical judgment is properly grasped — there will spring forth such an intense yearning for self-perfecting that it will prove satisfactory to every most daring conqueror, nay, it will help him attain the loftiest goal he has set for himself.
